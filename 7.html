Tribute to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India. Known as the People's President, he spent four decades as a scientist and science administrator, mainly at DRDO and ISRO.

Key Achievements

Played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs.

Project Director of India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III).

Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and Secretary of DRDO.

Recipient of the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
